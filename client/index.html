Instructions

This is a test to see if you can accuratly identify military viechles the same way a US ARMY Cavalry Scout has to. For each viechile you will be asked if it is friendly or enemy, and then you will be asked to select the correct name for it. Once you are done you can calculate your score by hitting the button at the bottom. After your score has been calculated you can save it by typing in a username and hitting the submit button.

Good luck!
